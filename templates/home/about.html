About Glance Job Fair 2.0

Welcome to Glance Job Fair 2.0, your premier platform for connecting students with top companies.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and employment by providing a seamless platform for job seekers and employers to connect.

Features:

  • Easy registration and profile creation
  • Direct communication with employers
  • Job matching based on skills and preferences
  • Real-time notifications for opportunities
